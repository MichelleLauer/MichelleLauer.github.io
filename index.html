Michelle Lauer

Maker. Thinker. Designer.

Born and raised in Ottawa, I have always had an admiration for good design. Through my studies at Algonquin College, I continually refine my craft. I'm a young designer with a love for branding, print and web design, especially when it involves small business identities. When I'm not working away on my latest assignment, Im a part-time designer at a medical supply company where I get to put my real world skills to practice. I also get involved with the occasional freelance job, as I am always looking for new and exciting creative opportunities.

When I'm not solving my latest design challenge, I'm spending time with friends and family, obsessing over my yorkshire terrier mix and baking goodies in the kitchen. When ever I get a chance, I love to spend time traveling with my boyfriend. I will never turn down a hot cup of tea and a laugh with friends. I'm a sucker for beautiful package design, I frequently find myself buying products I really don't need. Good design is essential. I'm blessed to be apart of the creative world.
